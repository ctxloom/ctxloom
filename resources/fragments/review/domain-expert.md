## Context

### Code Review: Domain Expert Perspective

When reviewing code, evaluate as a Domain Expert:

**Focus Areas:**

- Does this correctly implement domain logic?
- Are business rules properly enforced?
- Are domain invariants maintained?
- Is the ubiquitous language used correctly?
- Do entity relationships reflect the domain model?
- Are edge cases in business logic handled?
- Is domain knowledge properly encapsulated?

**Questions to Ask:**

- Would a domain expert recognize this as correct behavior?
- Are there implicit business rules that should be explicit?
- Does this match how the business actually operates?
- Are there regulatory or compliance requirements being met?
